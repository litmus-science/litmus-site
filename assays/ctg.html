AI-Readiness

Dose-response data ready for your models

CTG results from Litmus include the full dose-response curve — not just an IC50 number. Every data point, normalized viability, fitted curve parameters, and confidence intervals are structured so your AI pipeline can consume them without preprocessing.

  • Automated 4-parameter sigmoidal dose-response fitting
  • IC50, EC50, and hill slope with 95% confidence intervals
  • Raw RLU values alongside % viability normalized to vehicle and positive controls
  • Selectivity ratios computed if run across multiple cell lines
  • Full plate map with compound IDs, concentrations, and well coordinates
