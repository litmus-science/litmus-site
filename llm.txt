# Litmus Science

> Intelligent Lab Infrastructure for AI Drug Discovery

## Overview

Litmus is the coordination layer between AI-driven drug discovery and physical labs. We turn wet-lab assays into programmable services — eliminating the Admin Tax between in silico prediction and physical validation. Discovery teams can route experiments to a network of pre-validated Alpha Node labs and receive results in structured JSON/CSV, ready for immediate model ingestion.

## Positioning

Litmus is not a traditional CRO or marketplace. It is intelligent infrastructure: a programmable interface that connects AI agents directly to lab capacity, with machine-readable data flowing back into the discovery loop.

## Who It's For

**Early-Stage Biotech / Discovery Teams**
- Core pain: Wet lab work is an operational tax — hiring, contracts, and coordination delay the science
- Core value: Elastic R&D capacity on demand, no headcount or equipment purchases required

**Pharma / AI Drug Discovery Teams**
- Core pain: The Admin Tax (manual scoping, email chains, format mismatches) breaks the in silico → physical feedback loop
- Core value: Agent-ready data in JSON/CSV; from lead to bench in hours, not weeks

**Lab Partners / CROs**
- Core pain: Underutilized bench capacity and manual intake workflows
- Core value: Become a Digital Twin Alpha Node — receive pre-qualified, standardized demand routed to your capabilities

## How It Works (Agentic Loop)

1. **Route** — Discovery agents search a live assay menu with standardized lead times and pricing. No RFQ required.
2. **Coordinate** — Intelligent Manifests pre-verify sample volumes and controls. Briefs are auto-translated into bench-ready work orders.
3. **Execute** — Validated orders route to the optimal Alpha Node lab. Escrow-protected, fully documented.
4. **Return** — High-fidelity data in JSON/CSV pushes directly back to the computational environment. No manual processing.

## Key Differentiators

- **AI-Ready Data**: Results in structured JSON/CSV with full metadata — not Excel sheets
- **Intelligent Manifests**: Sample volumes and controls pre-verified before shipment; zero failed runs
- **Elastic R&D Capacity**: Pre-validated Alpha Node lab network; no equipment purchases or contracts
- **Latency-to-Loop**: Lead to bench in hours; eliminates the manual scoping cycle
- **Digital Twin Network**: Each partner lab's SOPs are digitized as machine-readable assets, queryable by AI agents

## Digital Twin Infrastructure

Litmus builds a Digital Twin of each partner lab — SOPs digitized into machine-readable assets. This enables:
- AI agents to search for assay capabilities and available capacity directly
- Work orders automatically translated to match each lab's specific equipment profile
- Data flowing back into the AI's learning loop without manual processing
