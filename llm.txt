# Litmus Science

> AI Wet Lab Validation Marketplace

## Overview

Litmus is a marketplace platform connecting scientific ideas to the lab capacity needed to test them. We enable anyone to run real wet lab experiments starting at $200, making scientific validation accessible beyond traditional CROs, cloud labs, and academic collaborations.

## Key Information

- **Minimum Experiment Cost**: $200
- **Safety Levels**: BSL-1/2
- **Default Policy**: Open science - results publicly shared
- **Platform Model**: Marketplace connecting Requesters with qualified lab Operators
- **Payment**: Escrow-based system with payment held until result approval

## How It Works

1. **Learn**: We teach users how to go from curiosity to testable hypothesis using AI tools and structured protocols
2. **Submit**: Users post experiments using standardized templates with instant pricing guidance
3. **Validate**: Qualified operators execute experiments with full documentation; payment held in escrow

## For Requesters

- Post experiment requests with clear protocols and acceptance criteria
- Get instant pricing based on complexity, materials, and privacy tier
- Choose privacy level: Open Access (default), Delayed Release (+10-20%), or Premium Private (+25-50%)
- Approve results before payment is released from escrow

## For Operators

**Requirements:**
- Verified institutional affiliation and active lab access
- PI approval or facility agreement for external work
- Demonstrated competency in claimed techniques
- Compliance with safety regulations and institutional policies

**Benefits:**
- Flexible hours - claim jobs that fit your schedule
- Supplemental income at competitive rates
- Build reputation with specialty badges
- Contribute to open science while earning

## Open Science Commitment

By default, all experiment requests and results are public to:
- Build a shared corpus of validated scientific data
- Enable reproducibility and prevent duplicate work
- Benefit the entire scientific community

Privacy tiers available for patent/publication timing or commercial R&D.

## Trust & Quality

- **Operator Verification**: Identity confirmation, institutional affiliation, authorization documentation, competency assessment
- **Documentation Standards**: Timestamped photos/videos, lab notebook entries, raw data with metadata
- **Escrow Protection**: Payment held until requester approves results
- **Spot-Check Duplicates**: Random experiments re-run by different operators for reproducibility verification
